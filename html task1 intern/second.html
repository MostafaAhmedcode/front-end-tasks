Spaghetti Carbonara

Overview

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta (or bacon), and black pepper. It's creamy, delicious, and very easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Cooking Steps

  1. Boil water and cook the spaghetti with a bit of salt.
  2. While the pasta cooks, fry the pancetta in olive oil until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, mix eggs, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.
  4. Drain the pasta and keep a little pasta water.
  5. Quickly mix the hot pasta with the egg mixture (the heat will cook the eggs).
  6. Add the pancetta and a little pasta water if needed for creaminess.
  7. Serve hot with extra cheese and pepper.
